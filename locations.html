Pok Pok located on SE Division is the original Pok Pok serving food found at pubs, restaurants, homes and the streets of Southeast Asia with the majority of the food coming from Thailand, and specifically from the North and Northeast of Thailand.

Pok Pok opens daily at 11:30am. If there is a wait, all parties arriving before 10pm will be seated. If you are on the waitlist, we suggest enjoying appetizers and beverages at our Whiskey Soda Lounge, located kitty-corner from our restaurant. Our hosts happily coordinate between the two locations.
